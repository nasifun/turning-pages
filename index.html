About the Series

"TURNING POINT" tells the story of a forgotten rebellion, hidden deep in the jungles of Africa. As secrets emerge, communities are forced to face their past and fight for their future. This TV series blends historical fiction, suspense, and Afro-realism in a raw, immersive journey.
