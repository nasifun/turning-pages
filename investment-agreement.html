This document outlines the digital terms and conditions applicable to investors participating in the crowdfunding initiative for the TV series titled "TURNING PAGES," hosted at www.turningpages.com.

Investors must be at least 18 years of age or legally represented by a guardian...

3. Digital Appearance in TV Series

Quarterly financial reports will be provided, with a 30-day clarification window.

5. Risks

Investors accept up to 80% loss risk in case of project underperformance.

6. Rights and Limitations

No ownership or creative control is granted; investment is strictly financial.

7. Termination

Refunds may be issued at the team's discretion, minus up to 5% admin fees.

8. Dispute Resolution

Mediation followed by arbitration applies to disputes.

9. Governing Law

This agreement follows international business law standards.

10. Digital Amendments

Any updates will be communicated via email or the website and require majority approval.

By participating digitally, investors accept all terms via the online confirmation process.
